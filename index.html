About Shreya

Hi, I'm Shreya Kaul (she/her), a dedicated Counselling Psychologist based in Delhi/NCR. With a deep passion for mental health and healing, I specialize in providing individual therapy sessions that create a safe, non-judgmental space for your personal journey.

My approach centers on individual therapy and healing, where I focus on providing a secure environment for individuals on their path to self-discovery and emotional well-being. I believe that every person deserves a space where they can explore their thoughts, feelings, and experiences without fear of judgment.

With extensive background in counseling psychology, I am committed to supporting you through life's challenges, helping you develop coping strategies, and empowering you to create positive changes in your life.

My Commitment to You

I am available for both individual therapy sessions and collaborative approaches to healing. Whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship issues, or simply seeking personal growth, I'm here to support you. Feel free to reach out via DM for sessions or collaborations.

My Services

Individual Therapy Sessions

Healing & Self-Discovery Programs
